### FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My model is broken, the hair is missing, etc.">
    Make sure to use **EEVEE** **renderer** instead of cycles.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My model has pink overlay on it, like there is material that missing after import.">
    Try to disable **Sphere Texture** and **Toon Texture** under Model Setup on MMD addons.
